Our Company

Parks Fashion is brand name owned by Kumardhubi Trading Private Limited. Kumardhubi Trading Private Limited also works in other fields. Our brand Parks Fashion specialize in manufacturing designer kurtis. Currently we are producing only womens wear.

OUR PLAN

Parks Fashion is currently operation in one region. But we are planning to expand all over india. We have a highly skilled fashion designer who are designing range of stylish ready made cloth. We are also planning to launch collection of stylish mens wear in coming season

OUR VISION

Our vision is to manufacture high quality product. We want to produce cloth which are designed by highly skilled fashion designer. Our vision is to provide high quality range of product to choose at an affordable rates.
